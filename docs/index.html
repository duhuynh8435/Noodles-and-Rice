Welcome

WHY NOODLES AND RICE?

Vietnam is an agricultural country which mainly produces rice and tropical agriculture products, and with their creativity, Vietnamese have different kinds of noodles based on rice. Therefore, “NOODLES AND RICE” is selected as a simple way to introduce Vietnamese cuisine to everybody.
At NOODLES AND RICE, you are able to enjoy most popular Vietnamese dishes which are cooked by Vietnamese chefs. We would like to bring freshness, aroma, and essences to your tongue to satisfy your senses.
Apart from dishes in our menu, every weekend we will introduce a weekend special which is an authentic Vietnamese dish. Please follow our facebook page to update the details.

WHAT IS VIETNAMESE CUISINE LIKE?

Vietnamese food is considered one of healthiest cuisines worldwide because:

  • It is the balanced combination between fresh herbs, vegetables, meat, carbohydrate, and a selective use of spices to reach a fine taste.
  • It is based on fresh ingredients, low quantity of fat within dishes, and low in sugar.
  • It is almost often naturally gluten-free, as many of Vietnamese dishes are made with rice, rice noodles, rice papers, and rice flour instead of wheat.
One of indispensable features in Vietnamese cuisine is dipping sauce. Each dish is served with each different dipping sauce, but the most popular one is fish sauce that is diluted slightly with a splash of lime juice, water, sugar, chilies, and garlic.
Vietnamese cuisines employ various types of cooking: stir-fry, steam, fry, boil, slow-cook, braise, ferment, etc.
